This Week's Cake!
 
  
 
  

What's Virtual Games and Cake without some cake? This week, we have a chocolate mug cake! If you've given it a go, we'd love to see some photos in the #food channel on our Slack workspace.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt, optional
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Splash vanilla extract, optional
  • 1 egg, optional
  • 3 tablespoons chocolate chips

Method

This recipe requires a microwave safe mug and a microwave.

Put the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt in a microwave-safe mug. Blend thoroughly with a fork. Add the milk, vegetable oil, vanilla and egg and blend until smooth. Stir in the chocolate chips.

Microwave on high for 90 seconds, then for 15 second intervals until cooked (Iâ€™d recommend putting a bowl or plate under the mug in case it spills out!). Let cool for 2 to 3 minutes before eating.

Adaptations

Can be made without the egg, vanilla and/or salt depending on your requirements.
