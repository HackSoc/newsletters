This Week's Cake!
 
  
 
  

For Virtual Games & Cake this week, try out a Tea & Spice Cake! Make sure you post your finished creation in the #food channel in Slack.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of raisins
  • 1/3 cup of margarine
  • 1 + 1/4 cup of tea
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 ts cinnamon
  • 1 ts nutmeg
  • 1/2 ts ginger
  • 1 pinch of cloves
  • 1 ts of dried rosemary
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 5 ts baking powder
  • 1/2 ts salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celcius
  2. Combine the tea, margarine, raisins, sugar, and spices in a small pan, bring to a boil and keep boiling for three minutes
  3. Set aside to cool
  4. In a mixing bowl, seive the flour, the baking powder, and the salt
  5. Once the liquids have cooled, add them to the mixture and combine
  6. Cook for ~40 minutes
