Talk Submissions are Closing Soon!
 
  
 
  

If you'd like to give an online pre-recorded talk to our members in the Spring term, then don't delay - submissions close on the 1st of January, so there's not much longer to submit your talk!

Remember that you don't need to have written your talk already; we only need an idea of what you'll be talking about in your submission.

If you'd like to find out more and make a talk submission, head over to the news article on our website!
